Communication
In our group we had a tough time with communication out of class time with scrums. I was present at all of the scrums we had, but other members in our group were absent and we couldn't get them to attend. This had a big impact on monitoring ticket progress through scrums and it was hard to see some team members progress unless we were all present in class. Two of our team members scot and coen left us at different stages of development which impacted on the performance of our group. The team had no idea where these members had gone. They made no effort to send us the work on their tickets or communicate with us regarding the matter. Better following of the Agile principle "Business people and developers must work together daily throughout the project." Would have allowed earlier actions to be implemented to make sure the tickets, that were uncompleted were done inside the duration of our sprints.
